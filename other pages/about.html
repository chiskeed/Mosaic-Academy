Our Story

Founded in 2011, Mosaic Academy embarked on a journey to redefine education, focusing on holistic development and fostering a culture of excellence.

From our humble beginnings in a small community to our current status as a pillar of academic achievement, we've embraced innovation and inclusivity every step of the way.

Over the years, we've celebrated numerous milestones, from the expansion of our campus to the introduction of cutting-edge programs designed to empower our students for success in a rapidly changing world.

But beyond the accolades and achievements, what truly defines our story is the unwavering dedication of our educators, the boundless curiosity of our students, and the support of our vibrant community.

As we continue to evolve and grow, our commitment remains unchanged: to inspire, to challenge, and to nurture the leaders of tomorrow.

Facilities and Resources

Modern Classrooms

Our state-of-the-art classrooms are equipped with the latest technology and ergonomic furniture to create an optimal learning environment for students.

Advanced Laboratories

Explore the world of science in our advanced laboratories equipped with cutting-edge equipment and resources, fostering hands-on learning and experimentation.

Computer labs with up-to-date hardware and software.

High-quality computer labs provide up-to-date hardware and software for computer science classes, coding projects, and research, fostering digital literacy and technological skills.

Libraries stocked with a wide range resources

High school libraries offer a vast collection of books, digital resources, and reference materials, along with quiet study areas and research assistance to support students' academic pursuits.

Gymnasiums with sports facilities for physical education

Featuring sports facilities for physical education classes, intramural sports, and extracurricular activities, gymnasiums promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship among students.

Cafeterias for nutricious meals

School cafeterias offer nutritious meals and snacks for students and staff, accommodating dietary needs and preferences while fostering a sense of community during meal times.

Visual arts studios equipped with art assets

Equipped with tools and materials for drawing, painting, sculpture, and other art forms, these studios encourage creativity and self-expression through visual arts education.
